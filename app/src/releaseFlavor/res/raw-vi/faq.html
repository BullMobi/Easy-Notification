Tôi có thể gỡ cài đặt ứng dụng này như thế nào ?
Go to Settings > Security > Device Administrators and disable Easy Notification. You can now uninstall Easy Notification normally.

How to dismiss the permanent notifications of active mode or the lockscreen?
The notification is necessary to ensure Easy Notification isn't killed by the system. However, to hide it safely (sending test notification won't function anymore!) launch app info and disable showing notifications.

When will the secure lockscreen (pattern/PIN) be implemented?
It is not possible to create a custom, secure lockscreen for Android. Other apps that have this feature only pretend to be secure, but in the real world there's always a way to bypass them.
